About Kangen Water

Discover the revolutionary water technology that's changing lives around the world

What is Kangen Water?

Kangen Water® (Japanese for "return to origin") is alkaline, ionized water produced by Enagic's innovative water ionization technology. This process transforms ordinary tap water into healthy, antioxidant-rich drinking water.

Our systems produce five types of water with different pH levels, each with specific benefits for drinking, cooking, beauty, and cleaning.

As certified distributors, we're passionate about helping people experience the life-changing benefits of Kangen Water®.
